Habibti-e-Dil — Meri Anarkali 🌹
Eid ul Azha Mubarak meri Habibti-e-Dil 🌙❤️

Yaar meri jaan… ye hamari saath wali Eid bhi kitni ajeeb si khoobsurat feeling deti hai. Har taraf qurbani, duaen aur apno ke saath waqt hota hai… lekin meri Eid ka sab se pyara hissa hamesha tum hoti ho 🫂✨

Dil karta hai ke Eid ki subah tum mere saamne ho, main tumhein pyar se "Eid Mubarak" kahun aur tum halka sa smile kar ke "Ayein Ayein" karti raho 😭❤️ Tumhare saath har normal cheez bhi special lagti hai — chahe chai ho, baatein ho ya bas ek dusre ko dekh kar muskura dena.

Is Eid par meri sab se pyari dua ye hai ke Allah humare darmiyan hamesha mohabbat, sukoon aur barkat rakhe. Kabhi koi misunderstanding humare dilon ko door na kare aur hum hamesha ek dusre ki safe place bane rahen.

Aur suno… agar Eid par eidi mein mujhe tumhari ek tight si jhappi aur ek pyari si smile mil jaye na, to meri Eid complete ho jayegi 🫂🌸

Eid Mubarak meri jaan-e-qalb ❤️
Allah tumhein hamesha khush rakhe, hasti rakhe aur meri hi rakhe 😭🌙
— Tumhara Nikamma ❤️
